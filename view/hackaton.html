EPHEC Hackathon 2023

In 2023, I participated in the EPHEC hackathon.

This weekend event tasked teams of 3 to 5 people with developing a project. It was my first foray into hackathons and proved to be immensely rewarding. I gained valuable insights into teamwork and time management.

Our project focused on sustainable development. We imagined a system that utilizes the heat generated by data centers to heat entire buildings.
