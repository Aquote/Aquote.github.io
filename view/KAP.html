KAP

For two years I was part of a KAP called 'Kot Planete Terre (KPT)'.

The KPT is a group of 15 over-motivated students who want to shake things up and introduce a zero-waste lifestyle to the students of Louvain-la-Neuve.

These two years of KAP have been really exciting in every sense of the word, from personal development to fun. It's been a great flood of emotions that I'll remember for a long time.

Our project focused on sustainable development, especially zero waste. This helps me to improve a lot of my soft skills such as sociability, professionalism, critical observation, self-awareness and so on.
